PrintWriter  class
---------------------
1) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter(System . out);
    What  does  object  pw  contain ? --->  Reference  of  console  i.e.  System . out

2) Where  is  PrintWriter  class  defined ?  ---> In  java . io  package

3) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter(System . out , true);
    pw . println("Hyd");
    What  does  the  statement  do ?  --->  Prints  "Hyd"  on  console  and   moves  to  next  line

4) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter(System . out , false);
    pw . println("Hyd");
    Is  "Hyd"  printed ?  ---> No  due  to  the  2nd  argument  false

5) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter(System . out);
    What  is  the  default  2nd  argument  of  PrintWriter  constructor ?  --->  false

6) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter(System . out);
    pw . println("Hyd");
    pw . close();
    Is  "Hyd"  printed ?  ---> Yes  due  to  pw . close()

7) PrintWriter  pw = new  PrintWriter("a.txt");
    pw . println("Hyd");
    What  does  the  statement  do ?  ---> Writes  "Hyd"  to  the  file  a.txt
