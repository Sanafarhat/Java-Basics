fill()  method
---------------
1) What  does  fill()  method  do ?  --->  Modifies  elements  of  array  with  a  different  value

2) What  are  the  four  arguments  of  fill()  method ?  ---> Array , start  index(inclusive) , end  index(exclusive)  and  new  element

3) Arrays . fill(arrayname , x , y , z)
    What  does  fill()  method  do ?  --->  Modifies  elements  of  array  from  indexes  x  to  y -  1  with  z

4) Why  is  fill()  method  called  thru  class  name  Arrays ?  --->  Since  it  is  a  static  method  of  Arrays  class
